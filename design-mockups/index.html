05 · from your reference

Amber Studio

A faithful rebuild of your "Pigma Editor" reference: near-black UI, warm amber accent, IBM Plex Sans + Mono, a real menu bar, Layers/Assets/Pages tabs, a full inspector (Transform → Auto Layout → Fill → Stroke → Effects → Typography → Export), and the local-first story woven through ("Offline-ready", "0 bytes uploaded", "Local history", "no upload"). The most production-complete of the set.
